## üåå what is this?

![wave](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Trilokia/Trilokia/379277808c61ef204768a61bbc69fea6d3b67de/bottom_wave.svg)

</div>

**BlocksByPi** reads your hand through a webcam, maps **21 skeletal landmarks** on it **30+ times per second**, and lets you **place, move, rotate, and destroy** virtual blocks ‚Äî with nothing but gestures.

No gloves. No sensors. No special hardware.

Just your hand, a webcam, and Python.
